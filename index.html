Three Neat Pieces of Pop Music

Death- Yes He's Coming

1970's rock band, Death, did more than just pioneer punk in Detroit, Michigan. They also dabbled in other sounds including uplifting, psychedelic gospel as revealed in their 2014 archival release, Death III.

Kryptonyte- (self titled)

If you like DJ Screw or southern rap tape grit, consider giving Kryptonyte's self-titled album a listen. This Dallas-based, 2018 release carries on and rejuvenates the Houston born tradition of intoxicating, lo-fidelity rap.

Lizzy Mercier Descloux- Payola

The linked track "Payola" is a chaotic mix of tantalizing grooves and melodious traffic by the late French artist, Lizzy Mercier Descloux.
